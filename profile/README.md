## üöÄ Hosted Pages

### Energy Planning & Infrastructure Tools

#### [OnSSET - Open-Source Spatial Electrification Tool](https://energy-modelling-tools.github.io/onsset/)

OnSSET is a GIS-based optimization tool developed to support electrification planning and decision-making for achieving energy access goals in currently unserved locations.

**Key Features:**
- Bottom-up optimization energy modeling
- Spatially explicit electrification analysis
- Support for conventional and renewable energy technologies
- Open-source and modular structure
- Cost-effective electrification planning

#### [OSeMOSYS - Open Source Energy MOdelling SYStem](https://energy-modelling-tools.github.io/osemosys/)

OSeMOSYS is an open-source energy systems optimization model for long-term energy planning. It is designed to address questions about energy transitions and policy.

#### [NISMOD - National Infrastructure Systems Model](https://energy-modelling-tools.github.io/nismod/)

NISMOD is a systems-of-systems modeling framework for analyzing interdependencies and long-term capacity requirements for national infrastructure.

#### [OnSTOVE - Open-Source Spatial Temporal Optimization of Value for Energy](https://energy-modelling-tools.github.io/onstove/)

OnSTOVE is a geospatial tool for assessing clean cooking solutions and analyzing the costs and benefits of different cooking technologies.

#### [PathCalc - Pathway Calculator](https://energy-modelling-tools.github.io/pathcalc/)

PathCalc is a tool for calculating and analyzing energy transition pathways and scenarios.

### Financial Planning Tools

#### [FinPlan - Financial Planning Tool](https://energy-modelling-tools.github.io/finplan/)

FinPlan provides financial planning and analysis capabilities for energy projects and infrastructure investments.

#### [MinFin - Ministerial Finance Tool](https://energy-modelling-tools.github.io/minfin/)

MinFin supports ministerial-level financial analysis and planning for energy and infrastructure sectors.

#### [FinTrack - Financial Tracking Tool](https://energy-modelling-tools.github.io/fintrack/)

FinTrack helps track and monitor financial flows and investments in energy projects.

#### [FFRM - Fossil Fuel Reduction Model](https://energy-modelling-tools.github.io/ffrm/)

FFRM analyzes strategies for reducing fossil fuel dependence and transitioning to renewable energy sources.
